Who it's for

For photographers who shoot a lot and work on a modest machine. Manoni is built to cull a big batch fast, make quick but real edits, and hand back clean copies — and to stay out of your way while you do it.

  • You shoot in volume and need to sort keepers from rejects quickly.
  • You want real edits — tone, colour, crop, heal, filters — without a heavy install.
  • You work on a weak or old laptop and want it to stay fast.
  • You care that your originals are never altered — culling only moves files, edits export copies.
The editor

Twelve tools on one rail

Click a tool on the right-hand rail to open its panel. Every edit is non-destructive — with undo, redo and a live histogram.

Basic

White balance, tone, and detail — clarity, texture, denoise, dehaze, sharpen — plus vibrance and saturation.

Color mixer

Per-hue HSL bands, plus dedicated gold and skin mini-HSLs so only that material moves.

Effects

Vignette, grain and split-tone for finishing a look.

Crop

Trim, straighten a tilted horizon, rotate and flip — with ratio and social presets.

Resize

Resize one photo, or every photo in a folder and its subfolders in one batch.

Perspective

Fix keystoning and straighten converging lines.

Heal & Clone

Remove blemishes automatically, or Alt-click to pick a clone source.

Focus blur

Blur the surroundings and keep your subject sharp.

Text & Watermark

Live text overlays — many per photo, snapped to any corner.

Logo

Drop a transparent PNG — with size, opacity, tint, flip and corner-snap.

Filters

Saved slider presets, previewed live on the current photo in a clickable strip.

Actions

Record a macro and replay it on one photo — or a whole folder.

Undo / redo · live histogram · dark & light theme with an accent colour · a Georgian / English interface.

Browse & cull

A virtualized thumbnail strip — thousands of files stay smooth — beside a nested folder tree. Arrow keys browse and cull each photo into keep / reject folders. Nothing is deleted: culling only moves files, and Ctrl+Z undoes any move.

Filters & the “Last” slot

A filter is a saved slider preset — one click applies the whole look. Save a photo and its edit is pinned as “Last” for the next shot. Export a filter group to a small .json and share your looks between machines and friends.

Export

Save a full-resolution copy as JPEG / PNG / WEBP — to an _edited/ subfolder or one fixed folder. Metadata is kept or stripped per your choice, and the original is never touched.

Keyboard

Hands stay on the keys

Browse and cull without reaching for the mouse. The Help button in the top bar opens a tabbed guide covering all of it.

EscCancel recording
EN · ქართული · +

The interface ships in English and Georgian — and adding your own needs no code and no rebuild. Settings generates a template of every phrase; translate the ones you want and import it back. Anything left untranslated simply stays English, so even a half-finished pack works the moment you load it.
