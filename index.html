Who it's for

For photographers who shoot a lot and work on a modest machine. Manoni is built to cull a big batch fast, make quick but real edits, and hand back clean copies — and to stay out of your way while you do it.

  • You shoot in volume and need to sort keepers from rejects quickly.
  • You want real edits — tone, colour, crop, heal, filters — without a heavy install.
  • You work on a weak or old laptop and want it to stay fast.
  • You care that your originals are never altered — culling only moves files, edits export copies.
The editor

Twelve tools on one rail

Click a tool on the right-hand rail to open its panel — click any card below to see exactly what it does. Every edit is non-destructive, with undo, redo and a live histogram.

Undo / redo · live histogram · before / after compare · dark & light theme with an accent colour · an English, Georgian & Polish interface.

Browse & cull

A virtualized thumbnail strip — thousands of files stay smooth — beside a nested folder tree. Arrow keys browse and cull each photo into keep / reject folders. Nothing is deleted: culling only moves files, and Ctrl+Z undoes any move.

Filters & the “Last” slot

A filter is a saved slider preset — one click applies the whole look. Save a photo and its edit is pinned as “Last” for the next shot. Export a filter group to a small .mnf file and share your looks between machines and friends.

Export

Save a full-resolution copy as JPEG / PNG / WEBP — to an _edited/ subfolder or one fixed folder. Metadata is kept, stripped, or converted to sRGB for the web per your choice, and the original is never touched.

Keyboard

Hands stay on the keys

Browse and cull without reaching for the mouse. The Help button in the top bar opens a tabbed guide covering all of it.

←→Previous / next photo
↑↓Keep / reject the current photo
CtrlOOpen a folder of photos
CtrlSQuick-save an edited copy
CtrlZUndo
CtrlYRedo
CtrlRShow / hide the pixel rulers
[]Shrink / grow the heal brush
RStart / stop recording an action
PReplay the action on the current photo
ShiftPReplay it over the whole folder
EscCancel recording
Community

Share language packs & filters

Translated the interface, or built a filter group you love? Post the file on Manoni’s Discussions board — and pull down what others have shared. Everything imports with a double-click, or from an Import button.

Language packs

Export your translation from Settings as a small .mnl file and post it. Untranslated lines fall back to English, so even a partial pack is useful.

How it works

Download a shared file, then double-click it — Manoni installs it. Or use Import: Settings for a language, the Filters panel for a group. To share back, export the file and open a new post.
